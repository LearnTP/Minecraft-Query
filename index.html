MCQ

MineCraft Query

Get any information from any Minecraft Server

Easy

You can output your information anywhere you want, with one line of code.

Customizable

We don't limit how you output returned information, so you can write your own scrips to do so. Alternatively, we have included a few functions to output for you, if you don't want to write your own.

Accurate

MCQ gets new information everytime the page is refreshed, to keep your information up to date.
